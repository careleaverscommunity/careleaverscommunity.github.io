COVID 19

Care Leavers are a marginalized group who often face significant social and economic disadvantage. With COVID-19 crisis, as people become more and more reliant on close family and friends support, Care Leavers with limited social networks faced increased social isolation and economic challenges, and also leading to mental health challenges.

The Impact of Covid-19 on Children's Residential Care Ireland

A survey was undertaken to assess the impact of COVID19 on staff and children in residential care. Focus was to document the challenges arising for children and staff as a result of the COVID19 restrictions, the impact on work practices, the need for increased or additional support and any innovative practices emerging as a result of the crisis.
